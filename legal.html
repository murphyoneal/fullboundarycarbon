Disclaimer

What this site is not

The content on fullboundarycarbon.org is offered for educational, research, and public-record purposes. It is not legal advice. It is not investment advice. It is not engineering certification of any specific project. Specifically:

  • The recomputed building numbers on the Corrected Ledger are analytical re-presentations of public disclosures under a wider accounting boundary. They are not certified life cycle assessments. They are not intended to substitute for an EN 15978 or ISO 14040/44 assessment performed by a credentialed practitioner for regulatory or procurement purposes.
  • The DRL framework is a working scientific methodology, not a settled or universally adopted standard. The framework is offered for review, citation, and disputation. Users who apply the framework to their own analyses do so under their own professional judgment.
  • Nothing on this site constitutes an accusation of fraud, deception, malfeasance, or knowing misconduct by any named individual, institution, or organisation. The work uses auditor's voice throughout: configuration is described from the institutions' own published documents; intent is not characterised.
  • Investment, procurement, regulatory compliance, or specification decisions based on the content of this site should be reviewed by appropriate professionals before action.

Privacy & data

fullboundarycarbon.org is a static-content website. It does not run user-tracking analytics; it does not set behavioural cookies; it does not require user registration.
