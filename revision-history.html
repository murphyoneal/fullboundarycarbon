Revision history

A complete version log of every material change to the documents and pages on this site. The log is part of the evidentiary standard the site is built to — if something changes, it is recorded here, with a reason.

Version Date Changes
v2.7 June 2026 Materials Timeline published (Companion Graphic No. 03). Interactive multi-material price index — softwood lumber (BLS WPU0811), structural steel (BLS WPU1017), aluminium (LME primary), cement (BLS WPU1321) — rebased to 1982 = 100, 1980–2026. Thirty-one sourced events across four categories (tariffs, conflicts, policy, promotion) rendered as toggleable overlays. Year-range dual slider. Plantation establishment window (1988–1997) and harvest window (2013–2030) shaded. Chart.js. Runs in any browser.
